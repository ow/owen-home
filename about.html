TL;DR

What

For the last few years, I ran a freelance UX writing and consulting business. It started with Charged in 2013, a weekly newsletter delivered to 15,000 people, and grew into helping others with words for more than three years.

Where

I live in Toronto, Canada, and previously lived in Amsterdam, The Netherlands with my partner and our fluffy scottish-straight cat, Mika.

My accent will betray me, however, as I grew up in Wellington, New Zealand, one of my favorite places in the world. We invented the flat white there.
