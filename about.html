I'm a technologist with experience wearing different hats, as a writer, developer, engineer and marketer.

Because I've been in the trenches as a developer *and* writer, I know all about the tech jargon that matters, from Node.JS to GCP.

Today, I help great teams get to their potential by making technology friendlier with words, and help product teams build their UX writing discipline, or position themselves in a crowded, jargon-laden startup world.

Oh, and I write a weekly column about how technology is shaping the world for Medium's OneZero, called Debugger.

TL;DR

What

I run my own business. It started with Charged in 2013, a weekly newsletter delivered to 15,000 people.

Today, it's also a paid daily service with 400 members and I deliver it Tuesday - Friday mornings. Oh, and helping people like you, too.

Where

I'm in the middle of moving to Toronto, Canada, but right now I live in Amsterdam, Netherlands with my partner and our fluffy scottish-straight cat.

I spent the better part of my life in Wellington, New Zealand, one of my favorite places in the world. We invented the flat white there.

Weird

When I'm not working, I love to read, bake and hike. I'm a little obsessed with finding new music, too.
